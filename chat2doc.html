Chat2Doc Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how the Chat2Doc Chrome extension handles user data.

Data Collection and Usage

The extension reads and processes the text content of your AI chat conversations only when you explicitly click the "Load Chat" button. This data is required to prepare your export.

All chat content are stored locally on your computer using the chrome.storage.local API. Your conversation data is never transmitted, uploaded, or saved to any external server or third-party service. It remains on your machine until you clear your browser's storage.

Your data is used exclusively for the core functionality of the extension:

  • To parse the chat content and separate user and AI messages.
  • To format the content into the export format you select (Word, Markdown, etc.).

The extension does not transmit any data to external servers or third parties. All processing occurs locally within the user's browser.

Cookies and Tracking

Chat2Doc does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies.

Changes to This Policy

As Chat2Doc does not collect any user data, we do not anticipate frequent changes to this privacy policy. If any changes are made, we will update this page accordingly.
